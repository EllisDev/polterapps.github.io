ToothTimer Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 3, 2024

Overview

ToothTimer is a simple tooth brushing habit app for children. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

ToothTimer stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Brushing history and streak information
  • Selected avatars and unlocked cosmetic items
  • Coin balance

What We DON'T Do

ToothTimer does NOT:

  • Collect any personal information
  • Send data to external servers
  • Track you across other apps or websites
  • Share data with third parties
  • Use analytics or tracking tools
  • Display advertisements

Data Storage

All data remains exclusively on your device. If you delete the app, all associated data is permanently deleted from your device.

Children's Privacy

ToothTimer is designed for children. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or anyone else.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.
